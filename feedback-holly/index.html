How the process went

The search

This was a competitive process. A talent pool of 163 candidates was identified, over 35 discovery conversations were held, 20 first interviews conducted and 9 competency interviews completed. Five candidates were recommended for final interview — and you were one of them.

Your interviews

Both your interviews were warm, fluent and genuine. Your cause connection to Somerset and its communities was some of the strongest in the process — specific, local, and clearly deeply held. Your trust and foundations track record is the strongest of any candidate in the field. Your Beacon CRM experience, having implemented it yourself at CHAT, was immediately practical and distinctive. Val in particular responded to your values and your connection to the communities Thrive serves.

Feedback from the client

What Thrive said about you

Matthew and Val spoke warmly about you. Your connection to the cause, your values, and your experience alongside village agents and talking cafes at Somerset Sight came through clearly and were genuinely valued.

The area where the panel felt less confident was around the structural and systems side of individual giving — the stewardship journey design, segmentation, and the infrastructure that sits behind a donor acquisition programme at scale. At points in the interview, when questions moved into that territory, the answers were slightly less defined than the panel needed to feel fully confident about building that infrastructure from a near-zero base on day one.

Matthew reflected that you almost certainly have all of that capability — it is present in everything you do at CHAT — but that in the room it did not come through with quite the clarity and confidence the panel were looking for at this stage of Thrive's fundraising journey. Those are fine margins and they do not diminish what you brought to the process.

Looking ahead

A development observation

The feedback points to one area worth reflecting on for future interviews in individual giving-led roles: being ready to talk with equal confidence about the systems and process side of fundraising — stewardship journeys, segmentation, data management, acquisition mechanics — as you do about relationships and values. The capability is there. It is about making sure it comes through clearly in a formal setting.

Moving forward

You should feel proud of how you presented yourself throughout this process. You were in a genuinely strong field and you were recommended for final interview for very good reasons. That says a great deal about your calibre.

Please do stay in touch. I have very much enjoyed working with you and I would genuinely love to work with you again when the right opportunity comes along.
